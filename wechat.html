What is WeChat

WeChat is one of the world's most popular social media messaging apps, and is similar to WhatsApp. WeChat supports video, voice, and text chat and has unique features like localized translation.

Learn More

More About WeChat

  • It was founded in 2011 by chinese.
  • Available in 17 different languages.
  • Features: Text messaging, hold-to-talk voice messaging, broadcast (one-to-many) messaging, video calls and conferencing, video games, image and video sharing, and location sharing are all available on WeChat.

    HOW DOES IT WORKS

  • Each WeChat user is identified by a unique barcode known as a QR code.
  • To add another user to WeChat, one person can scan their QR code. You may also add a person and search for persons nearby by using a phone number or ID.
  • WeChat is a popular means for Chinese people to communicate.
  • Also works as a payment app.
    		•

    HOW WECHAT ALOGRITHM WORKS

  • WeChat filters photographs in Moments using two separate algorithms: a visual-based algorithm that filters images that are visually similar to those on an image blacklist and an Optical Character Recognition (OCR)-based algorithm that filters images with sensitive content.
