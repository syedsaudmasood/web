What is WhatsApp

WhatsApp is a free, multiplatform messaging app that lets you make video and voice calls, send text messages, and more — all with just a Wi-Fi connection. With over 2 billion active users, WhatsApp is especially popular among friends and family who live in different countries and want to stay in touch.

    HOW DO THEY WORK BEHIND THE SCENES

  • To communicate with the clients, WhatsApp employs a heavily modified version of XMPP on an Ejabberd server (more on that later).
  • The XMPP client connects to the WhatsApp servers via an SSL socket.
  • Until the client opens or reconnects to this socket to retrieve the messages, all sent messages are queued on the servers.
